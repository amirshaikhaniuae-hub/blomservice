Home / About Us

Our story

Built on showing up.

Blom Service B.V. was founded in Amstelveen with one standard: when a contractor calls, a qualified worker is on site — not eventually, but on schedule.

Who we are

A staffing partner that treats reliability as the product.

Blom Service B.V. is registered in Amstelveen, the Netherlands, and supplies construction labour and delivery crews to contractors across the country. The company was incorporated to bring discipline and accountability to a trade where last-minute gaps cost real money — every placement is screened, briefed, and backed by a same-week replacement guarantee.

What we stand for

Three commitments behind every placement.

Reliability

A confirmed start time is a commitment, not an estimate. If a worker can't make it, we find cover before the shift starts.

Vetted People

Every worker is screened for trade competency, certification and right-to-work before they're matched to a site.

Straightforward Process

One coordinator, one phone call, no chasing paperwork through three departments to change a shift.
